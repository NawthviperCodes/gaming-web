Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

How do I add coins to my account?
You can top up your balance using the "Top Up" buttons on the dashboard. We accept various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency.
What games are available on GameZone?
We offer a wide variety of popular games like Pong, Space Invaders, Tetris, Snake, and many more. Our game library is constantly expanding with new titles being added regularly.
How do I create an account?
Click the "Sign Up" button in the top-right corner of the homepage. Fill in your details, verify your email address, and you're ready to start playing!

Account & Billing

How do I reset my password?
Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect all payment information. We never store your complete credit card details on our servers.
Can I request a refund?
Refund policies vary depending on the purchase. Please contact our support team through the Contact Us page with your order details for specific refund inquiries.
